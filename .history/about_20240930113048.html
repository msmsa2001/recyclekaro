Process

Our E-Waste Recycling Process

01

Collection and Sorting

We gather e-waste from various sources and sort it into categories for safe handling.

02

Dismantling

Electronic devices are taken apart to extract useful components and remove hazardous materials.

03

Shredding and Granulation

Separated parts are shredded into smaller pieces to facilitate recycling.
