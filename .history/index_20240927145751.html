    Frequently Asked Questions

    What types of electronics do you recycle?

    We recycle computers, laptops, mobile devices, servers, networking equipment, and more, ensuring responsible disposal of all e-waste.

    How do I ensure my data is secure before recycling?

    We offer certified data destruction services to completely wipe all personal or sensitive information from your devices before recycling.

    Do you offer pickup services for recycling?

    Yes, we provide convenient pickup services for bulk collections and businesses. You can also drop off smaller quantities at our recycling center.

    Why should I recycle my electronics?

    Recycling helps reduce e-waste, conserves natural resources, and ensures that harmful materials are safely managed, contributing to a cleaner environment.
